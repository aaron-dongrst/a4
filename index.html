Team Identity

Norse History

The Vikings were founded in 1961 and have built a rich history of consistent elite competition, legendary players, and passionate fans.

The Bank

The Vikings call U.S. Bank Stadium in Minneapolis, Minnesota home. It is one of the most modern stadiums in the league.

Skol Nation

The Vikings have some of the most passionate fans in the NFL, known for their intimidating Skol chant before each game, creating one of the most powerful atmospheres in the league.

The Face of the League

Vikings receiver Justin Jefferson, drafted in 2020, has quickly become one of the most popular players in the league. Known for his elite route running, reliable hands, and consistent positive attitude, his accomplishments include 2022 Offensive Player of the Year, 2x First Team All-Pro, and 2x Second Team All-Pro. In 2024, he signed a 4-year extension reported at $140 million, making him the highest-paid non-quarterback at the time.

More About the Vikings

Ownership

The Vikings consistently have one of the highest-ranked ownership groups in the league, including ranking 2nd out of 32 in the 2025 NFLPA players poll.

Purple Legends

The Vikings have been home to some of the NFL’s greatest players, such as Randy Moss, Fran Tarkenton, Paul Krause, Alan Page, and Adrian Peterson.
